About the Organization

Red Apple Performing Arts, Inc. is a nonprofit performing arts organization dedicated to promoting cultural understanding, community connection, and artistic excellence through dance, performance, education, and public cultural programming.

The organization operates the Red Apple Dance Group and serves the community through performances, cultural events, and arts-related nonprofit activities.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire, connect, and serve the community through performing arts, cultural exchange, and educational programming that celebrates heritage, creativity, and inclusion.
